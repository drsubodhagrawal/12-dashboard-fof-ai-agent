13 Q&A entries · Athens Sleep · Sleep Medicine
What are your office hours?
We are open Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM. We are located at 125 King Avenue, Suite 200, Athens, Georgia. Free parking is available in the lot.
FAQApproved
Do you accept new patients?
Yes, we are currently accepting new patients for sleep medicine consultations and sleep studies. A referral from your primary care provider is required for most insurance plans.
FAQApproved
How do I schedule a CPAP resupply?
You can request CPAP supplies by calling our AI number anytime. Aria will collect your patient ID, CPAP model, and supplies needed. Our clinical team processes requests within 1-2 business days.
CPAPApproved
What insurance plans do you accept?
We accept most major insurance plans including Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, Humana, and Medicare. Please call to verify your specific plan.
BillingApproved
What should I expect during a sleep study?
An in-lab polysomnography (PSG) takes place overnight. You will arrive at 8:30 PM, be set up with monitoring equipment by 9 PM, and typically leave by 6 AM. The study is completely painless and you may bring your own pillow and comfortable clothing.
SchedulingApproved
How do I get my CPAP pressure adjusted?
CPAP pressure adjustments require a provider review. Please call to schedule a CPAP follow-up appointment with Dr. Agrawal. Bring your CPAP device or your myAir/DreamMapper data if available.
CPAPApproved
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Same-day cancellations or no-shows for sleep studies may incur a $50 fee. Please call as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.
SchedulingApproved
How do I request a prescription refill for sleep medication?
Prescription refill requests are reviewed by Dr. Agrawal. Please call or send a request through the patient portal. Allow 2-3 business days for processing. We cannot refill controlled substances by phone.
RxApproved
Can I do a home sleep test instead of in-lab?
Yes, home sleep tests (HST) are available for patients with a low-to-moderate risk of sleep apnea. Dr. Agrawal will determine which study type is most appropriate during your initial consultation or based on your referral.
SchedulingApproved
Where are you located and is there parking?
We are located at 125 King Avenue, Suite 200, Athens, Georgia 30606. There is free parking in the lot adjacent to the building. We are off the Loop 10 bypass, easily accessible from Athens and surrounding areas.
FAQApproved
How do I check if my sleep study results are ready?
Sleep study results typically take 7-10 business days to be reviewed by Dr. Agrawal. You will receive a call or letter when results are ready. You can also check through the patient portal or call our office.
SchedulingApproved
